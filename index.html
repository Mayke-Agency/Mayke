Mayke Creative Agency

About

At Mayke, we partner with founders and businesses to bring brands to life — shaping ideas into distinct and enduring identities. After over 15 years of experience across the creative and marketing industries, we felt called to create something different.

Founded in Laguna Beach, California, we began by working closely with local entrepreneurs and small businesses in our community. Today, Mayke is a bi-coastal creative studio partnering with brands across hospitality, wellness, beauty, and lifestyle — helping founders build brands with lasting impact.

What We Do

We work with founders and teams to shape brands from the inside out — developing identity, digital presence, and creative systems that allow businesses to grow.

Our work spans brand strategy, identity design, digital experiences, campaign production, and experiential marketing, supporting brands from early concept through ongoing evolution.
